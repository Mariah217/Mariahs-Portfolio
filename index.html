Flicks Movie Generator (group project)

Flicks Movie Generator was created to minimize the amount of time spent searching for a movie to watch. There are so many different streaming services now-a-days that it can be overwhelming and quite time conusming trying to find a movie to watch. With Flicks Movie Generator, you can simply select a genre from the dropdown menu and it will generate 5 random movies for you to chose from. The next time you visit the website, it will remind you which movie genre was last searched.
